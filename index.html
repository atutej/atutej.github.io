Atula Tejaswi Neerkaje

I am an Undergraduate Student studying Computer Science at Manipal Institute of Technology, and a researcher with Conversational AI and Social Analytics Lab, University of Marburg.

In the summer of 2022, I was a Mitacs Globalink research intern at Université du Québec à Montréal, supervised by Prof. Marie-Jean Meurs. I was also an undergraduate researcher at Project MANAS, the official AI and Robotics team of Manipal Institute of Technology.

Research Interests

My general interests are in Deep Learning, Natural Language Processing, and Reinforcement Learning. I am interested in building models insipired by cognitive and psychological theories, that can generalize better and adapt to uncertain scenarios through a higher level understanding of their environment.

In the application side, I am interested in a broad range of domains including Computational Social Science, Multimodal Learning, Financial NLP, and Privacy Preserving Deep Learning. I have been fortunate to contribute to a wide range of research directions, and my work in these areas has been published at venues such as ACL, AISTATS, ICWSM, ACM SIGIR, and many more.
